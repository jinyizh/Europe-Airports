Interactive Map of European Airports

Airport data were imported from OpenFlights, which represent the name, location, codes and altitude of airports in Europe as in 2017. Name changes after that are still being updated manually by me.

Notes: consideration of "European" countries:

  • Armenia: Geographically in Asia, but has historical/cultural association with Europe, and was a member of the former USSR.
  • Azerbaijan: Straddles the border between Europe and Asia, but has historical and cultural association with Europe, and was a member of the former USSR.
  • Cyprus: Geographically in Asia, but has strong historical and cultural association with Europe.
  • Georgia: Geographically in Asia, but has historical/cultural association with Europe, and was a member of the former USSR.
  • Kazakhstan: Straddles the border between Europe and Asia and was a member of the former USSR, but historically and culturally a Central Asian country. Not shown in this map.
  • Russia: Straddles the border between Europe and Asia but historically and culturally an European country. This map shows its European part.
